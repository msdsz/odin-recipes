Baked Sweet & Sour Chicken

This baked sweet and sour chicken is very savory sweet and the chicken gets wonderfully crispy

Serve over rice just like in your favorite Chinese restaurant

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup olive oil, divided
  • 4 boneless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 4 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 6 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1.5 quart cooked white rice
